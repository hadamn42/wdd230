Membership Level

NP Membership

  • This membership level is for local non profit organizations.
  • This membership level is equal to:
    • One full Bronze Membership
    • One half Silver Membership
      (You pick the half!)
  • Yearly fee: None

Bronze Membership

  • Yearly fee: $1000
  • Entry into the Business Spotlight Lottery
  • 2% discount to booth rental during events
  • Free knife handle
  • 5 whole hot dogs (no buns)

Silver Membership

  • Yearly fee: $5000
  • Everything from the Bronze Membership
  • 5% discount to booth rental during events
  • Access to paid trainings
  • Free knife blade
  • 5 extra votes in Chamber meetings
  • 10 whole hot dogs (buns included)

Gold Membership

  • Yearly fee: $10,000
  • Everything from the Bronze Membership
  • 5% discount to booth rental during events
  • Access to free trainings
  • 30 free ritual summons
  • Free knife
  • 20 extra votes in Chamber meetings
  • 15 whole gold-plated hot dogs (buns also gold-plated)
